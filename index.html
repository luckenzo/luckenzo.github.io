Biography

Lucky Onwuzurike is a PhD student in the Information Security Group at University College London (UCL) under the supervision of Dr. Emiliano De Cristofaro and Dr. Gianluca Stringhini. My research interests include: security and privacy on online social networks, Android security, network security, and applied cryptography. Prior to starting my PhD, I obtained an MSc in Information Security at UCL and a BEng in Computer Engineering at the University of Maiduguri, Nigeria. I also have experience as Web developer and Network Engineer.
